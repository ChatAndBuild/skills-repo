# Hugging Face Paper Publisher

Publish and manage research papers on Hugging Face Hub. Supports creating paper pages, linking papers to models/datasets, claiming authorship, and generating professional markdown-based research articles.

## Instruction
- Initialize the publication process by creating a dedicated paper page on the Hugging Face Hub using the paper ID or URL.
- Configure metadata to link the paper page with corresponding models, datasets, and spaces to build a cohesive project ecosystem.
- Generate or format professional Markdown articles that summarize the research findings for the community.
- Implement authorship claims by linking the paper to the correct user profiles or organizations.
- Update paper details periodically to reflect new citations, related works, or updated model checkpoints.
- Utilize Hub features to engage with the community through comments and discussions directly on the paper page.

## When to Use
- When sharing new AI/ML research papers with the Hugging Face community to increase visibility and adoption.
- When building a "project hub" that requires linking a preprint or paper to its technical artifacts (code/data).
- When creating accessible, markdown-based versions of research papers for Hub-native reading.

## Output
- A summarized plan for Hub publication including linking strategies and metadata configuration.
- Formatted Markdown content ready for paper page descriptions or blog posts.
- A checklist of precautions for authorship verification and metadata accuracy.
